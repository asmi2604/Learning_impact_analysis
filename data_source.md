# Data Source

**Dataset:** Open University Learning Analytics Dataset (OULAD)  
**Source:** https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rocki37/open-university-learning-analytics-dataset  
**License:** CC-BY-SA-4.0  
**Size:** 32,593 students | 7 CSV files | 10M+ clickstream events  
**Citation:** Kuzilek J., Hlosta M., Zdrahal Z. (2017). Open University 
Learning Analytics dataset. Scientific Data, 4:170171.

## Why this dataset
- Contains both demographic AND behavioural clickstream data
- Real production-scale data from a major university
- Peer-reviewed and published in Nature Scientific Data
- Enables both outcome prediction AND content effectiveness analysis
